if the exam is obligatory and when it will be?

Once you have completed both Part 1 and Part 2 of this training you are eligible to take the Microsoft Azure 
Fundamentals certification exam at no cost. It is not mandatory, but we encourage you to get Microsoft certified. :) 

The exam does not have a fixed date. You can schedule it at your convenience, but your discount is valid for 90 from 
the event attendance date. You must schedule and take the exam within 90 days from the event date. For more guidance, 
see Demo video: How to redeem your discounted exam 

    https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/videoplayer/embed/RE4DoHP
